Masala Dosa

Description: A delicious South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with spicy mashed potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. Soak rice and dal overnight. 2. Grind to a smooth batter. 3. Ferment overnight. 4. Heat a pan, spread the batter, add filling, and cook.

Podi Idli

Description: Steamed rice cakes served with a spicy podi (powder) made from lentils and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup idli batter
  • 2 tbsp podi
  • Coconut for garnish

Instructions:

1. Prepare idli batter. 2. Grease idli molds. 3. Pour batter and steam for 10-12 mins. 4. Serve with podi and coconut.

Ven Pongal

Description: A comforting rice and lentil dish tempered with spices and ghee.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Ghee for tempering

Instructions:

1. Cook rice and dal together. 2. Temper spices in ghee. 3. Mix tempering with cooked rice and serve.
